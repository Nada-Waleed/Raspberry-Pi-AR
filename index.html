Raspberry Pi!!

A mainstay in the world of makers and electronics, The Raspberry Pi® is a single-board, low-cost, high-performance computer first developed in the UK by the Raspberry Pi Foundation. Not only has it helped bring the joy of electronics and computer programming to people around the world, but it has also become a staple of the maker community. The ever-present Pi in its latest iteration—Raspberry Pi 4 Model B—as the Pi Foundation continues to improve on an already excellent product. There’s also the five-dollar Pi Zero – a game-changing Raspberry Pi in a much smaller package – as well as its WiFi-laced cousin, the Pi Zero W.
